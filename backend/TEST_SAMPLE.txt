COVERAGE

This policy provides coverage for Third Party Liability as mandated under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. The coverage includes:

1. Third Party Bodily Injury: Unlimited liability for death or bodily injury to any third party person.
2. Third Party Property Damage: Coverage up to Rs. 7,50,000 for damage to third party property.
3. Personal Accident Cover: Coverage of Rs. 15,00,000 for the owner-driver in case of death or permanent total disability.

The policy covers legal liability arising out of the use of the insured vehicle in a public place.

OWN DAMAGE

This policy does not include Own Damage coverage. For comprehensive coverage including Own Damage, please refer to our Comprehensive Motor Insurance Policy.

THIRD PARTY

Third Party coverage is provided as per the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. The insurer shall be liable to indemnify the insured against all sums which the insured shall become legally liable to pay in respect of death or bodily injury to any person or damage to property of a third party caused by or arising out of the use of the insured vehicle.

EXCLUSIONS

The following are excluded from coverage under this policy:

1. Damage to the insured vehicle itself (Own Damage)
2. Loss or damage due to war, nuclear risks, or terrorism
3. Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
4. Use of vehicle for racing, speed testing, or illegal purposes
5. Consequential loss or depreciation
6. Mechanical or electrical breakdown
7. Damage occurring outside the geographical limits of India

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. The insured must hold a valid driving license at all times.
2. The vehicle must be used only for the purpose stated in the policy.
3. Any modification to the vehicle must be reported to the insurer.
4. The insured must take all reasonable steps to prevent accidents.
5. In case of an accident, the insured must notify the insurer immediately.
6. The policy is non-transferable without prior written consent of the insurer.

LIMITATIONS

1. Coverage is limited to use within the geographical territory of India.
2. The policy does not cover damage to the insured vehicle.
3. Personal accident cover is limited to the owner-driver only.
4. Third party property damage is limited to Rs. 7,50,000.

CANCELLATION

The policy may be cancelled by the insured with written notice. No refund of premium will be provided if cancellation is initiated by the insured. If cancelled by the insurer, a pro-rata refund may be applicable subject to terms and conditions.

RENEWAL

This policy can be renewed before the expiry date. The insured must provide all necessary documents and pay the renewal premium. Late renewal may result in inspection of the vehicle and additional charges.

CLAIM PROCEDURE

1. Immediately inform the insurer or the nearest branch office
2. Do not admit liability or make any payment without insurer's consent
3. File an FIR with the police if required by law
4. Submit all required documents including claim form, FIR copy, driving license, RC copy, and repair estimates
